About

MBTA Transit Tracker is the last app that you will ever need for using the MBTA. It provides you with various ways to view the real-time locations of all routes, stops, and vehicles for any bus, subway, or commuter rail route. Prediction times are available for all stops, and the predictions are pulled directly from the MBTA to give you the most accurate arrival times possible.
